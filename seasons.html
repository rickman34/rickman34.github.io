The seasons of the year are based off where the Earth is on the tilted axis and it's orbit around the sun. In Spring & Autumn, the sun isn't tilted towards the sun basically side-on with the sun. Summer, the earth's axis is pointing at the sun. Lastly, in Winter the northern hemisphere is tilted away from the sun.

Seasons are unpredictable!
